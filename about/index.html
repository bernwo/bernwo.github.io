Student, MSc Applied Physics @ Delft University of Technology, NL

2020 — present

I am on a 2 year Master's programme at Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), fully funded under the QuTech Scholarship. Recently, I joined a research group @ QuTech, namely the Borregaard Group, where I will be working on my Master's thesis, which is about quantum communication. In particular, I will be looking into how hybrid protocols involving quantum error correction code concatenation and entanglement purification can improve the performance of 3rd generation quantum repeaters.

2017 — 2020

I spent 3 years in City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK) under the prestigious HKSAR Top Scholarship studying both applied physics (major) and computer science (minor). In addition, I spent 3 months on an internship in McGill University, CA as well as 6 months on an exchange to KTH Royal Institute of Technology, SE. My final year project in CityUHK involves computational electromagnetism, and with my supervisor and co-workers' help the associated work has been published on a reputable journal.
